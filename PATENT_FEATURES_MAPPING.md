## âš ï¸ IMPORTANT NOTES

### 1. **File Patent BEFORE Public Launch**
- Patent requires novelty (not publicly disclosed)
- Your 500-user test was under NDA âœ…
- File patent before removing NDA
- Priority date = filing date

### 2. **Patent vs Copyright vs Trademark**
- **Patent:** Protects the METHOD/SYSTEM (how it works)
- **Copyright:** Protects the CODE (actual implementation)
- **Trademark:** Protects the NAME/BRAND ("LetsBunk")
- **Trade Secret:** Protects SPECIFIC ALGORITHMS (keep confidential)

**You need ALL FOUR for maximum protection!**

### 3. **International Protection**
- India patent: Protects in India only
- PCT filing: Extends to 150+ countries
- File PCT within 12 months of India filing
- Cost: ~$3,000-5,000 for PCT
- Then enter national phase in specific countries

### 4. **Enforcement**
- Patent gives you RIGHT to exclude others
- You must actively monitor for infringement
- Send cease & desist letters
- License or litigate
- Patent lasts 20 years from filing date
